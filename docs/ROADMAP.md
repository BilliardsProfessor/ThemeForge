# Theme Forge Roadmap

## Vision

**Theme Forge is a design-system workshop.**

Theme Forge helps designers and developers create cohesive, accessible design systems through experimentation, visualization, and education.

Rather than simply generating themes, Theme Forge encourages exploration by allowing users to see the impact of design decisions in real time, evaluate accessibility, and export reusable design tokens for a variety of platforms.

The project is intentionally:

- Framework-free
- Build-tool-free
- Dependency-light
- Educational
- Accessible
- Easy to understand
- Easy to maintain
